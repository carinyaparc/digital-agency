# Confidence scoring

Score each section of `brand-voice.md` when writing or reviewing.

## Levels

| Level | Criteria |
| ----- | -------- |
| **High** | 3+ corroborating sources, or explicit written guidance |
| **Medium** | 1–2 sources, or strong inference from patterns |
| **Low** | Single source, inferred only, or conflicting data |

## Overall confidence

| Overall | Rule |
| ------- | ---- |
| High | Most sections High; no unresolved conflicts |
| Medium | Mix of High/Medium; minor gaps documented |
| Low | Multiple Low sections or unresolved conflicts |

## Open questions

When confidence is Low or sources conflict:

- Document under "Open questions for team discussion"
- Include **agent recommendation** — turn ambiguity into confirm or override
- Tag priority: High (blocks completion), Medium, Low

## Privacy

Downgrade confidence if examples require redaction. Never expose customer PII in
guidelines.

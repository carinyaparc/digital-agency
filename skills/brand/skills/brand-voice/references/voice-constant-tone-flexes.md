# Voice constant, tone flexes

## Model

- **Voice** = personality and values — constant across all content. Anchored by
  the "We are / We are not" table in `brand-voice.md`.
- **Tone** = formality, energy, technical depth — flexes by audience and channel.

Do not change voice attributes per channel. Adjust tone dimensions instead.

## We are / We are not

Each row pairs a positive attribute with its counter (what to avoid):

| We are | We are not |
| ------ | ---------- |
| Confident | Arrogant |
| Approachable | Casual or sloppy |

Minimum four rows in generated guidelines; five to seven is ideal.

## Tone-by-context matrix

Three dimensions per context:

| Dimension | Question |
| --------- | -------- |
| Formality | How formal or casual? |
| Energy | How much urgency or enthusiasm? |
| Technical depth | How detailed or accessible? |

Cover at minimum: cold outreach, proposals, social media. Add customer success
and internal comms when sources support them.

## Enforcement order

When applying voice in **enforce** mode:

1. Apply voice constants (We are / We are not, terminology, messaging pillars)
2. Look up context row in tone matrix
3. Generate content
4. Validate against prohibited terms and anti-patterns
